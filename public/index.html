Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about PayFlow Insights.

Does this process real payments? ↓

No — PayFlow Insights is purely a simulation. No real payments are processed and no real accounts are created. Visitors complete a realistic-looking checkout form, then see a disclosure screen explaining it was a simulation. The goal is to measure real buying intent before you build.

Who is this for? ↓

PayFlow Insights is built for founders, product managers, indie hackers, and creators who want to validate demand before investing in a full payment integration. It's also useful for UX researchers testing checkout flows with real users.

Is it really free? ↓

Yes, completely free. No credit card, no account required. Just enter your email to receive your simulation link and private dashboard — that's it.

What checkout types are supported? ↓

Four types: one-time payment, subscription (recurring billing), physical product with a shipping address section and editable shipping cost, and account creation with password fields. Each renders a realistic checkout UI your potential customers interact with.

How do I see my results? ↓

When you create a simulation, you receive a private dashboard link by email. It shows total visits, checkout completions, conversion rate, and leads who opted in to be contacted. You can export leads to CSV anytime.
